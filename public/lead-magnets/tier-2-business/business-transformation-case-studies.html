🎯 Common Success Factors

✅ What Drives Success

  • Professional Assessment: Custom plant selection based on space analysis
  • Strategic Placement: Plants positioned for maximum impact and health
  • Maintenance Programs: Regular professional care ensures plant health
  • Employee Buy-in: Leadership support and communication about benefits
  • Phased Implementation: Gradual rollout prevents overwhelming staff
  • Metrics Tracking: Regular measurement of defined success indicators

❌ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Wrong Plant Selection: Choosing plants unsuited for office conditions
  • Inadequate Maintenance: Neglecting professional care requirements
  • Poor Placement: Plants in high-traffic or low-light areas
  • No Clear Goals: Implementing without defined success metrics
  • Rushed Timeline: Installing too many plants too quickly
  • Insufficient Budget: Cutting corners on quality or maintenance
